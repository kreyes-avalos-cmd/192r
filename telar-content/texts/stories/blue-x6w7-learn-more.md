Daily Nexus nov. 18,1991 
During the early 90s, AIDS drugs had severe side effects. The medicines did help, but they also affected patients in a way that made it difficult to continue normal activities in their daily lives. Dr. Anthony Fauci hoped for newer drugs that would replace Retorivir and DDI; however, he still believed that effective treatment was still far away.
